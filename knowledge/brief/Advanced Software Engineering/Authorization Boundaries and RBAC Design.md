### What This Covers

This topic explains how to design access control that stays manageable across modules, roles, and fine-grained permissions.

### Why It Matters

Products with approvals, payroll, HR data, or admin tooling need clear authorization boundaries to avoid unsafe access and confusing policy logic.

### Key Idea

Good RBAC design combines reusable roles with explicit permission checks and context-aware rules where business scope matters.

## Summary

Authorization should reflect real business responsibility, not just technical route protection.
