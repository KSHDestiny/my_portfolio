### What This Covers

This topic focuses on modeling business workflows with explicit states, transitions, and approval rules.

### Why It Matters

ATS, expense, KPI, and payroll features all become easier to reason about when transitions are controlled instead of loosely updated.

### Key Idea

Treat statuses as a real workflow model with allowed transitions, actor rules, and side effects.

## Summary

Well-defined state machines reduce invalid states and make approval-heavy systems more reliable.
