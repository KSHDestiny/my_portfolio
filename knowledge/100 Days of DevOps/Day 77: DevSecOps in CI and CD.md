### Objective

Understand the core idea behind DevSecOps in CI/CD and how it supports reliable DevOps delivery in real environments.

---

## Why It Matters

DevSecOps in CI/CD helps teams build, ship, operate, and improve systems with more consistency, safety, and visibility. In production work, this topic usually connects directly to automation, scalability, or operational reliability.

---

## Key Focus Areas

- Learn the main concepts and where devsecops in ci/cd fits in the delivery lifecycle.
- Understand the operational risks, common failure points, and safe defaults.
- Practice a small workflow that can be repeated in a real project.

---

## Practical Steps

1. Review the purpose and common use cases for DevSecOps in CI/CD.
2. Set up a small hands-on example or lab.
3. Observe the inputs, outputs, and operational behavior.
4. Write down common commands, configs, or checks used during troubleshooting.
5. Capture the production lessons that matter most for repeatable delivery.

---

## What to Watch Carefully

- Misconfiguration that works in development but breaks in production.
- Security, access, or secrets handling gaps.
- Missing visibility for debugging, alerting, or rollback.
- Processes that are manual when they should be automated.

---
